A place where you can take quizzes of your group of peope in the most interactive way as possible. Don't worry about where you or your people are. Just make the quiz happen!

Our Crazy Features

The platform is dedicated to make the life of quiz for the teachers and as well as the student easier and simpler. It gives some of the key features which solve many problems!

- The quiz can be taken from anywhere of the world, by the teacher as well as by the students.

- Specific timeline for the quiz can be specified as well as specific time limit can be set up for each question papers.

- The quiz app will give access the teachers to create multiple types of questions, such as short questions, essays, fill in the blanks and many more!

More Features

The platform not only focuses on the quizes and exams but also keeps an eye on the growth of the student's skills and knowledge!

- The teachers will be able to upload practise questions.

- Students can access practise questions provided by the teachers any time they want and will also be able to see peer solutions.

- The students will be able to see there daily quiz growth with the help of graphs.

- The students will be able to see their previous quiz questions and also their ansers so that they can learn from their mistakes.
